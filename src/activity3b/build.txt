1. There are two java programs you need to run.
2. Open TextUI.java and run the program.
3. It will shows two lines in terminal: three different tempreatures (Celsius,Fahrenheit, Kelvin) and two pressure measurements (Inches and Millibars) in textUI and keep updating the numbers.
4. Open SwingUI.java and run the program.
4. It will show three temperatures and two pressure measurements in one row and keep updating the numbers.
